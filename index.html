Recipe Roulette!

Enter Recipes: Type or paste your list of recipes in the provided input area, with each recipe on a new line.

Spin the Wheel: Click the "Spin the Wheel" button to randomly select a recipe.

View Recipe: The selected recipe will be displayed. If you don't like the choice, you can click the "Spin Again" button to choose a different recipe.

Background Music: Enjoy the background music while you use the site. Use the mute/unmute button to control the music.
